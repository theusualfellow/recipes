Description

Lasagna is a hearty, layered Italian dish made of wide pasta sheets, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. The layers are typically built with a rich tomato-based sauce with ground beef, and a delicious cheese blend of ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan. Baked until golden and bubbly, Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, perfect for family dinners and gatherings.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (boiled and drained)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and sauté until softened (about 5 minutes).
  2. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.

2. Make the Béchamel Sauce:

  1. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk to form a smooth paste (roux).
  2. Gradually add the milk while whisking continuously, ensuring no lumps form. Bring to a simmer, and cook for 5-7 minutes until thickened.
  3. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper, and remove from heat. Set aside.

3. Assemble the Lasagna:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  3. Layer 3 lasagna noodles on top, followed by more meat sauce, a layer of béchamel sauce, and a sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers until the dish is filled, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella cheese.

4. Bake the Lasagna:

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  3. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

5. Serve:

  • Garnish with fresh basil if desired, and serve with garlic bread or a side salad for a complete meal.
